# AetherLink Enterprise Support Plan

## Overview

AetherLink provides comprehensive enterprise-grade support for organizations deploying our AI Operations Brain platform. Our support plan ensures maximum uptime, rapid issue resolution, and proactive optimization of your AI-driven NOC operations.

## Support Tiers

### Enterprise Support (Included with Enterprise Plan)
- **24/7 Priority Support**: Round-the-clock access to senior engineers
- **Dedicated Technical Account Manager**: Single point of contact for all support needs
- **Response Time SLA**: 1 hour for critical issues, 4 hours for high-priority
- **Quarterly Business Reviews**: Strategic planning and optimization sessions
- **Custom Training**: On-site or virtual training for your operations team
- **Advanced Onboarding**: White-glove setup and configuration assistance

### Professional Support (Included with Professional Plan)
- **Business Hours Support**: 8 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday through Friday
- **Response Time SLA**: 4 hours for critical issues, 8 hours for high-priority
- **Technical Account Manager**: Shared TAM for account management
- **Annual Business Review**: Strategic planning session
- **Standard Training**: Virtual training sessions included
- **Guided Onboarding**: Remote setup assistance

### Standard Support (Included with Starter Plan)
- **Business Hours Support**: 8 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday through Friday
- **Response Time SLA**: 8 hours for critical issues, 24 hours for high-priority
- **Community Access**: Access to customer community and knowledge base
- **Self-Service Resources**: Comprehensive documentation and tutorials
- **Email Support**: Response within 24 hours

## Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

### System Availability
- **Enterprise**: 99.9% uptime SLA with financial credits for breaches
- **Professional**: 99.5% uptime SLA
- **Starter**: 99% uptime SLA (best effort)

### Response Times
| Severity | Enterprise | Professional | Starter |
|----------|------------|--------------|---------|
| Critical (System Down) | 1 hour | 4 hours | 8 hours |
| High (Major Feature Impact) | 4 hours | 8 hours | 24 hours |
| Medium (Minor Feature Impact) | 8 hours | 24 hours | 48 hours |
| Low (General Questions) | 24 hours | 48 hours | 72 hours |

### Resolution Targets
- **Critical Issues**: Resolution within 24 hours
- **High Priority**: Resolution within 72 hours
- **All Other Issues**: Best effort resolution
