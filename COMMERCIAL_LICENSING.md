# AetherLink AI Operations Brain - Commercial Licensing

## Overview

AetherLink Command Center is offered through multiple deployment and licensing models designed to meet the needs of organizations from startups to enterprise-scale operations. All models include the complete AI Operations Brain with self-learning capabilities, autonomous actions, and comprehensive audit trails.

## Deployment Models

### 1. SaaS (Software as a Service)
**Best for:** Organizations wanting zero infrastructure management with guaranteed uptime and automatic updates.

- **Fully managed cloud deployment** on enterprise-grade infrastructure
- **Automatic scaling** based on operational load
- **99.9% uptime SLA** with enterprise support
- **Daily backups** and disaster recovery
- **Multi-region deployment** options

### 2. Self-Hosted (On-Premises/Private Cloud)
**Best for:** Organizations with strict data sovereignty requirements or existing infrastructure investments.

- **Docker/Kubernetes deployment** packages
- **Air-gapped deployment** support
- **Custom integrations** with existing monitoring stacks
- **Flexible scaling** within your infrastructure
- **Data residency** compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)

### 3. Enterprise Hybrid
**Best for:** Large organizations needing both cloud and on-premises capabilities.

- **Multi-cloud deployment** with hybrid connectivity
- **Federated operations** across data centers
- **Advanced security** features (encryption, access controls)
- **Custom SLAs** and support agreements
- **Professional services** for implementation
